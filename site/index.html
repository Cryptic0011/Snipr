Everything between “grab that” and “done”

One tool for the whole loop: capture, mark up, extract, ship.

⌘⇧4

Area capture

Drag with an 8× loupe, live pixel coordinates, and window-edge snapping. Or press T and type exact dimensions.

⌘⇧5

Window & full screen

Pick a window Screenshot-app style or grab the whole display — always at native Retina resolution.

scroll

Scrolling capture

Scroll through a long page while Snipr stitches the frames into one seamless, tall image.

⌘⇧6

Screen recording

Region recordings as .mov with system audio, a live HUD, and a trim pass before you save.

⌘⇧O

OCR to clipboard

Point at pixels, get text. Vision-powered recognition lands straight on your clipboard — no popup, just a haptic.

edit

Annotate & ship

Arrows, shapes, highlight, blur, pixelate, step badges, text — with undo/redo and crop. Then drag it anywhere.
